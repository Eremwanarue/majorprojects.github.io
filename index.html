Comprehensive Analysis of Road Traffic Accident

The task focused on analysing road traffic accident data to identify patterns, risk factors, and impacts, and to develop recommendations for improving road safety. The tool used for this analysis was Microsoft Excel, which was employed to import, clean, and standardize the dataset, apply filters for exploration, compute key performance indicators (KPIs), and visualize results through dashboards. The findings revealed that road accidents occurred more frequently in November and on Fridays, with urban areas being particularly affected due to higher traffic volumes associated with weekend activities. Based on these insights, the analysis recommended measures such as enhanced traffic control at junctions, consistent illumination of traffic lights and roadside lighting, and regular road maintenance and debris removal. The study concluded that data-driven strategies can significantly aid in reducing accidents and improving public safety.

FREELANCERGIG ECONOMY

The task involved analysing freelancer data from multiple gig economy platforms to uncover trends in earnings, job success, and performance metrics. Tableau was the primary tool used, enabling data importation, cleaning, table joining, and visualization through interactive dashboards. The analysis explored key indicators such as earnings by job category, success rate by platform, and hourly rates across experience levels. Forecasting techniques were applied to predict future earnings trends. Findings revealed that specialized fields like graphic design and app development yielded higher pay, while experienced freelancers with strong client satisfaction records achieved higher rehire rates. The study recommended focusing on high-demand categories, enhancing client relationships, and leveraging forecasting insights for career planning. Ultimately, the Tableau dashboards provided clear, data-driven intelligence to support freelancers, businesses, and policymakers in optimizing performance within the growing gig economy.

Zico sales performance

Zico Mart, a leading retail company aiming to understand its sales performance to enhance business growth and customer satisfaction. The task was to analyse customer data to identify sales trends, top-performing product categories, and the influence of demographics such as age, income, and region on buying behaviour. The tool used was Microsoft Excel, utilizing pivot tables, charts, and slicers to build an interactive dashboard that visualized key insights. Analysis of DermAI Diagnostics Skin Cancer Dataset

The analysis centred on DermAI Diagnostics, a company aiming to improve early detection of skin cancer by analysing real-world clinical data on 1,089 skin lesions and associated patient risk factors. The task was to design and query a structured SQL database combining Patient Info and Lesion Info tables to explore how demographics, environmental exposures, and lesion characteristics relate to different diagnostic outcomes. The tool used was SQL, which was applied to import the dataset, define tables, create relationships via joins on patient id, and run analytical queries to summarize trends and segment patients and lesions. The findings from these SQL analyses highlighted meaningful patterns such as correlations between age, skin type, family or personal cancer history, pesticide exposure, and lesion behaviours (growth, colour change, bleeding, elevation) and the likelihood of particular lesion diagnoses thereby supporting more informed biopsy decisions, early detection strategies, and the creation of machine-learning ready data for AI-driven dermatology.
